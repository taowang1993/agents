## Timeline Overview

## Key Intellectual Turning Points

| Turning Point | Trigger Event | Before | After |
|--------------|--------------|--------|-------|
| **Philosophy to CS** | Cornell Philosophy BA → Harvard CS PhD | Pure humanities thinking | Philosophy + programming intersectional perspective — always concerned with "why" not just "how" |
| **Academia to painting** | RISD and Florence after PhD | Academic path | Understood the maker's way of working: not plan then execute, but discover in the process of making |
| **Painting to entrepreneurship** | Founded Viaweb | Artist identity | Entrepreneur identity, but retained maker's work philosophy |
| **Entrepreneurship to writing** | Left Yahoo | Doer | Thinker and writer. Discovered essays were his true medium |
| **Personal writing to institution building** | Founded YC | Independent thinker | Tried to systematize and scale his startup experience |
| **Institution building back to individual** | Stepped down from YC | Organizational manager | Returned to independent thinker. Recognized he wasn't suited to managing organizational growth |
| **Silicon Valley to England** | Moved to England, 2016 | In the center of Silicon Valley | Physical distance brought thinking distance. Away from noise made his essays deeper, slower, more fundamental |
| **New positioning in the AI age** | 2024-2026 AI wave | Focused on startups and programming | Began deep thinking about AI's impact on writing, thinking, taste, startups. Core view: AI age taste > execution, writing ability = thinking ability |
